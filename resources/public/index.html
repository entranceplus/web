R. V. College of Engineering (RVCE), Bangalore

R.V. College of Engineering (RVCE), established in 1963, is one of the earliest self-financing engineering colleges in the country, affiliated to VTU. The institution is run by Rashtreeya Sikshana Samithi Trust (RSST). RVCE is today recognized as one of India’s leading technical institutions.

M. S. Ramaiah College of Arts, Science and Commerce, Bangalore (MSRCASC)

M. S. Ramaiah College of Arts, Science and Commerce offers courses in Art (BA), Computer Science (B.Sc - Computer Science / Electronics & Mathematics, BCA - Bachelor of Computer Application)

National Institute of Technology, Delhi (NITD)

The National Institute of Technology Delhi (abbreviated NIT Delhi or NITD), is a public engineering institute located in New Delhi, India. It was established in 2010. NIT Delhi also offers M.Tech. in ECE, CSE, Mechanical Engineering and Applied Sciences. NIT Delhi also offers Ph.D. programs in various fields of engineering.
